One of my other hobbies is brewing beer, and I love to share and get feedback from friends and other brewers

Recent Brews

Blue Moon Clone

For this brew, done in Jan 2019, I used a Blue Moon clone kit but I doubled up on the marmalade for extra oranginess. The result was a bit like a wheat sour as the orange flavor is strong but a bit tart. Over all it was well received and with 5 gallons left in the keg I have pleny to bottle for picnics this summer!

Belgian Dubbel

For this brew, November 2018, I used a Chimmay Red kit to brew just over 10 gallons. This was the first time using my new brew pot and a new type of belgian yeast. The results were a very nice banana bread aroma and a very drinkable beer for 9.1% ABV. The beer did turn out a bit sweet and I think a bit more time fermenting to clean up residual sugars could have been beneficial. I'll age a few bottles for a year and see how they turn out.
